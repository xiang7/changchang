NARRATIVE

For the works under this category, they are all created on real information and data. Rather than simply visualizing the facts, the designer needs to think deliberately about the issues going on in the landscape field.

Generally speaking, this is a process for the designer to identify the problems/ issues first and then manage ways to get the related information and finally visualize them and collaborate them well. Ideally, to find more relationship behind this and get people to think.
